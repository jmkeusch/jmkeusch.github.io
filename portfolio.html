My Favorite Restaurant

Bistrot du Coin

This is an American-French bistro-style restaurant in my neighborhood in Washington, DC. It features some classic decor like the white-and-red checkered table cloths and vintage accoutrement on the walls and ledges. You'll see a variety of people there, including some of the neighborhood's French speakers. Bistro du Coin is a great place to celebrate a birthday while being conveniently located and reasonably priced.

Favorite Food

Beef bourguignon

Following our theme, beef bourguignon is also a French-American style dish featuring beef and vegetables braised in a red wine-based stew. It's an amazing dish to serve in the fall and winter with rich flavor that will fill the house with an excellent aroma. It can be served over mashed potatoes or egg noodles and goes great with the wine you used in the stew!
